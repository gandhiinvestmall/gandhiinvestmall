Types of ME Gold

  • Digital Gold

    Digital gold allows you to buy and own gold electronically. You can invest in gold in small quantities, starting from as low as 1 gram, and it is securely stored in insured vaults on your behalf. This form of gold investment is accessible, hassle-free, and does not require physical storage or handling.

  • Gold SIP (Systematic Investment Plan)

    A Gold SIP allows you to invest in digital gold regularly, just like a mutual fund SIP. You can contribute a fixed amount every month to purchase gold, making it a disciplined and systematic way to build wealth over time.

  • Gold ETFs (Exchange-Traded Funds)

    Gold ETFs are investment funds that hold physical gold and trade on stock exchanges. Investing in a Gold ETF gives you exposure to gold's price movements without having to buy or store physical gold. It's an indirect way to invest in gold while keeping your investment liquid.

  • Gold Bonds

    Gold bonds are government-issued bonds that offer returns linked to the price of gold. These bonds allow you to invest in gold in a paper format, earning interest in addition to potential capital appreciation from the gold price.

  • Physical Gold (Delivery Option)

    If you prefer owning physical gold, you can choose to take delivery of your gold after investing digitally. The physical gold will be delivered in the form of bars or coins, based on your choice.
